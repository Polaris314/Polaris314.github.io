How many recognizable people are in the picture given.

You will count the amount of people for which you can see more than half their head.

In other words, if more than half of their head is blocked out or outisde of the picture they are not to be counted.

Furthermore, included here is a way to make counting easier. Whenever a picture is clicked a small red circle will be drawn on the spot clicked and a counter below the picture will be incremented. This is only meant to facilitate the counting process, not be a replacement for it. You are still expected to fill in the answer.
